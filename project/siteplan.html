Community Mental Health & Addiction Support - Site Plan

This document outlines the planning for a website dedicated to supporting mental health awareness and addiction recovery within the community.

Site Name

Solace

This name represents a community-focused website that provides mental health resources and addiction recovery support. It is simple, professional, and memorable.

Optional domain availability: solace.org

Site Purpose

The website provides a hub for community mental health education and addiction support by offering:

  • Informational articles on depression and mental wellness
  • Resources for drug addicts seeking recovery programs
  • Tips for family and friends to support affected individuals
  • Interactive elements like quizzes and dynamic resource lists
  • Contact forms for users to request guidance or help

Scenarios

  1. As a visitor feeling depressed, I want to find helpful coping strategies so I can improve my mental health.
  2. As a family member of a drug addict, I want to locate local addiction recovery programs so I can guide them toward support.

Color Schema

Selected colors for the website:

  • Main Background: #f4f4f4
  • Primary Accent: #2c3e50 (Headings & navigation)
  • Secondary Accent: #e74c3c (Buttons & highlights)
  • Text: #333333 (Body text)

Typography

  • Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif – Clear and professional headings
  • Body text: 'Arial', sans-serif – Readable for all users
  • Special Sections: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – For callouts or quotes
